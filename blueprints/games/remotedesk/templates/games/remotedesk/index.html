Frequently asked questions

How do I connect my phone to my PC? +

Simply sign in with the same account on both devices. Your devices will automatically appear in each other's device list. Tap any online device to connect instantly.

Do I need to be on the same WiFi? +

No! While same-network connections offer the lowest latency, Glint also supports P2P and relay modes for remote access from anywhere in the world.

Is it free during the beta? +

Yes. Glint is currently free during beta testing. We're gathering feedback to improve the product before the official launch.

Is my connection secure? +

Absolutely. All connections use end-to-end AES-256 encryption. Your streaming data never passes through our servers unencrypted.
