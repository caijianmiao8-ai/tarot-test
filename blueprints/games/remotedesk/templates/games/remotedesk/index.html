Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Glint.

How do I connect data sources to Glint?
Use our prebuilt integrations to ingest product telemetry, support tickets, and monitoring alerts. You can also post custom events to our REST/GraphQL API, then map them to teams and severity levels in minutes.
Can Glint run securely for distributed teams?
Yes. Glint supports SSO, audit logging, role-based access, and regional data storage. You control which sources, teams, and environments each workspace can access.
Do you offer a free plan?
We offer a generous free tier for early teams, plus paid plans with higher event volumes, SSO, and audit features. Contact us for enterprise deployment and support.
Does Glint support mobile and desktop equally?
Absolutely. The same live feed, AI triage, and collaboration tools work on iOS, Android, macOS, and Windows so teams stay aligned wherever they are.
How is Glint different from generic alerting tools?
Glint pairs live customer signals with AI context and human collaboration. Instead of pages of noisy alerts, you get curated threads, co-browsing, and response rooms that keep customers informed while teams fix issues faster.
