Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Glint.

How do I connect my phone to my PC?
Simply sign in with the same account on both your phone and PC. Your devices will automatically appear in each other's device list. Tap any online device to connect instantly—no IP addresses or manual configuration needed.
Do I need to be on the same WiFi network?
No! While same-network connections offer the lowest latency, Glint also supports P2P and relay modes for remote access from anywhere in the world. The app automatically chooses the best connection method.
Is it free during the beta?
Yes. Glint is currently free during the beta testing period. We're gathering feedback to improve the product before the official launch.
Is my connection secure?
Absolutely. All connections use end-to-end encryption. Your streaming data never passes through our servers unencrypted, and we don't store any session recordings.
What are the system requirements?
Mobile: iOS 14+ or Android 8+
Desktop: Windows 10/11, macOS 11+
Network: Minimum 5 Mbps upload on host PC
Can I use it for gaming?
Yes! Glint is optimized for low-latency gaming. On LAN connections, you can expect low latency—perfect for strategy games, RPGs, and even some action games.
