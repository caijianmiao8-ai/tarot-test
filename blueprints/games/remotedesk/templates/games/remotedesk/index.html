Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Glint.

How do I connect my phone to my PC?
Simply sign in with the same account on both your phone and PC. Your devices will automatically appear in each other's device list. Tap any online device to connect instantly—no IP addresses or manual configuration needed.
Do I need to be on the same WiFi network?
No! While same-network (LAN) connections offer the lowest latency (5-15ms), Glint also supports P2P and relay modes for remote access from anywhere in the world. The app automatically chooses the best connection method.
Is it really free forever?
Yes. Glint Phase 1 (mobile-to-PC control) is completely free with no subscriptions, time limits, or hidden fees. We're building this for the community, and core features will always remain free.
Is my connection secure?
Absolutely. All connections use end-to-end AES-256 encryption. Your streaming data never passes through our servers unencrypted, and we don't store any session recordings. Your privacy is our priority.
What are the system requirements?
Mobile: iOS 14+ or Android 8+
Desktop: Windows 10/11, macOS 11+ (Big Sur or later)
Network: Minimum 5 Mbps upload on host PC for smooth streaming
Can I use it for gaming?
Yes! Glint is optimized for low-latency gaming. On LAN connections, you can expect 5-15ms latency—perfect for strategy games, RPGs, and even some action games. We support virtual gamepad controls and keyboard mapping.
How is this different from TeamViewer or AnyDesk?
Glint is designed specifically for mobile-to-PC control with gaming and productivity in mind. We offer lower latency through intelligent P2P connections, a mobile-first touch interface, and it's 100% free with no commercial-use restrictions.
What if I need help?
We have comprehensive documentation, an active Discord community, and email support at support@glintdesk.com. Check our GitHub issues page for known bugs and feature requests.
